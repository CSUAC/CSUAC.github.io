About Us

 Computer Science Undergraduate Advisory Council is determined to improve the Computer Science B.S., B.A., and Minor by providing a bridge for students to give input to the department through weekly meetings and projects. Additionally, the group aims to help students exceed by providing insight to industry internships, offering occasional mock-interview programs and providing resources to help students achieve their goals within the department.
